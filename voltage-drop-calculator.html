How it works:

  • Voltage drop is calculated using Ohm's law: V = I × R
  • Cable resistance depends on material, length, cross-section and temperature
  • Recommended maximum voltage drop: 3% for lighting, 5% for power circuits
  • Three-phase calculations use line-to-line voltage and √3 factor
